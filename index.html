Questions

The questions we get asked most.

Who owns all of this once it's built?+
The website and all the data behind it are yours to keep. The agents and booking flow run as part of your monthly service, and there's no lock-in — you can cancel any time, and you'll never be tied into a long-term contract.
What happens when your voice agent gets a call it can't handle?+
It never guesses. If a caller asks something it isn't sure about, it passes them straight to your team instead of making it up.
How do the intake forms work?+
They ask the right questions up front and drop qualified enquiries straight into your CRM, so your team only spends time on the ones worth chasing.
Do we have to change the way we already work?+
No. Your booking assistant and follow-up system plug into the CRM, calendar, and inbox you already use. Your team carries on exactly as they do now — except with a lot more time to focus on the tasks that actually matter.
Is our client data safe?+
Yes. Every agent runs on private, encrypted infrastructure, and we never feed your client data into shared models.
Once it's built, are we left on our own?+
No. The monthly fee covers the cost to keep your agents running and up to date.
What does it cost?+
It depends on the service, but most projects are a one-time setup fee plus a monthly fee. The monthly fee covers the servers that run it and any hands-on adjustments along the way. All costs are known and agreed upon ahead of time.
What if we're not happy with it?+
Our promise: we build it, and if you don't like it, you don't pay.
Where are you based?+
Toronto, Canada. We work with clients across North America.
